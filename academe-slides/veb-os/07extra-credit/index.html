LECTURE SLIDE CREATION

OVERVIEW

This activity is OPTIONAL, but a great opportunity to earn extra points by helping enhance our class materials. You will create PowerPoint slides based on the lectures and textbook chapters below.

These slides will support future review and may even be used during class presentations so your creativity and clarity will be highly appreciated.

WHAT TO DO

Create well-designed slides that:

  • Visualize the key concepts from each lecture

  • Break down topics into understandable sections

  • Optionally include animations or illustrations

  • Reflect not only the textbook content but also our lecture discussions

  • I trust you have taken some notes

Think of this as a way to reinforce your understanding while contributing to the class!
